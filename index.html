About


“What if you could scan this airtime into your phone?” A game-changing question posed by Mthunzi Xulu in 2013 after a study session at the UKZN Cafeteria. This question would set him on a yet unexplored path in the digisphere.

Over the next seven years, friends would mention Air Scan in passing with the hopes of it one day materializing. That one day finally arrived in 2020. A turbulent year that needed an app to breathe some fresh air in the app market place.

Air Scan prides itself in being a user-friendly app that does what it needs to do, so users can do what they have to do instead of fussing over dialing in digits. Forget dialing. Scan it with Air Scan.

Steps

Step 1

Select Mobile Network (Vodacom, MTN, Cell C, Telkom, FNB)

Step 2

Select "Scan Airtime", "and Scan Now."

Step 3

Position voucher in the rectangle a.k.a "Air Space." If the voucher is blurry, rotate the phone to scan in landscape.

Step 4

Select "Scan Voucher," holding the voucher steady, until scanning is complete.

Step 5

Check that the scanned airtime voucher pin numbers match those on the print voucher.

Step 6

Accept to recharge with the scanned airtime voucher. Rescan, or manually make any changes to the captured airtime voucher on screen if there are any errors.

Step 7

With more time on your hands, get back to doing what matters most - connecting and communicating!
